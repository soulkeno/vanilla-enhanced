About Escape Road 2

Escape Road is back with Escape Road 2! Get ready for the thrilling sequel that takes you on heart-pounding chasing and escaping gameplay! Your mission? Navigate the crowded roads, escape from the police, and survive! It's now updated with more vehicles, upgrades, and environments!

What is Escape Road 2

Escape Road 2 is the second version of the famous Escape Road game that brings players to an endless chase from police officers. However, this chase will be much more intense and dramatic than the original version. The police have been improved to be faster and smarter to chase you. They are more determined, have better weapons, and employ more sophisticated tactics. It's a driving game that puts players behind the wheel in a thrilling race against the police. Master high-speed maneuvers, outsmart relentless chasers, unlock new powerful vehicles, and escape!

How to play the Escape Road 2 game

Drive, smash, escape

As you may anticipate from a thrilling chase game, the rules of Escape Road 2 are extremely straightforward but challenging. Your main goal is to drive, escape, and survive for as long as you can. Your pursuit will be relentless, and the power of the police cars and military helicopters will only increase.

All you must do is to drive your car at maximum speed while avoiding objects like cars and buildings. When your automobile reaches its maximum speed, it can even destroy other cars. The objective is to survive as long as possible. If the police vehicles get you, the game is over. Race against the police and take a spot on the leaderboard. 

Unlock new vehicles

Along the race, you should collect and accumulate cash bags that appear randomly on the street. Then, you can use them to unlock a lot of amazing new cars in the shop. From Common to Rare to Epic to Legendary, there are up to 90 new cars that are just waiting for you to unlock. There are also armored tanks, race cars, luxury supercars, and much more.

New features added

Compared to the original version, Escape Road 2 game has been improved with many new features, promising to bring players a better experience. This game is equipped with more modern new cars along with more attractive new graphics. There are also many vehicle customization options present. Players can also get their hands on various environments. 

What's more fantastic?

In this part, players can get out of the car if it's stuck and find a new car to get in and drive! And when your vehicle explodes in the air, you can get out of it with a parachute and then land on the ground. After that, you can find a new vehicle and escape. So look around and hop on any vehicle that you find on the street and start racing against the police.

There are even more impressive features that we recommend you to find out yourself! 

Game Controls

To start and turn: Use A/D or left/right arrows

To drive: Use A/S/D or Left/Down/Right arrows.

To enter or exit a vehicle: Press the Spacebar.

Police cars will be pursuing you as soon as your automobile leaves the bank. The Arrow keys can be used to control the car's direction. There are numerous roads and automobiles in this city.

That's what's obstructing your escape path. Keep an eye out and move quickly to dodge all the cars on the road.

Since your car will stop if you collide with another vehicle. The police car will come racing in and encircle you to capture you right away. So, be careful and clever!

The game can be played at https://escaperoad2.io/ on your web browser of your Mobile and PC devices. No download is required!

Highlighted Features of Escape Road 2

3D graphics environment: Experience a genuine, immersive city atmosphere in the 3D Street Environment, where 3D graphics bring intense action to life.

Fast speed: Take part in the most amazing, fast-paced gameplay possible as you navigate the city's streets and attempt to outrun the cops in furious pursuits.

Vivid sound: Adjust dramatic sound effects, such as sirens and motors roaring, to keep the chase exciting.

A huge number of unique cars: Unlock a vast array of automobiles, ranging from standard cars to luxury supercars, each with its special performance and control.

The ability to hop on and exit any car: Players can get out of the vehicle and then get in any nearby car!

Parachute feature: With this feature, you don't have to worry about losing the game if your vehicle is in the air. You can use the parachute to land safely. 

Completely free: This is a free online driving and escaping game that you can play right on a web browser of any device. No download is needed.

Tips and strategies to survive the police chase

Avoid driving in congested areas as much as possible. There will be many challenges and obstacles in this busy city.

Buy upgrades to increase your car's power as well as test out different driving experiences.

Make abrupt turns instead of driving straight to divert the attention of the police. Also, it will help you avoid dead ends.

Get out of the car then find and enter a nearby car quickly if you get stuck. Be flexible to survive.

Other variations to try

The game comes with several spin-offs and each has new graphics and features!

  • Escape Road: The Original game
  • Escape Road Winter: Join the escape in the winter!
  • Escape Road City: Navigate in the bustling city and streets
  • Escape Road Halloween: Survive the Halloween night!
