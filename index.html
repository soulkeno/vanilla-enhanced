About Escape Road 2

Escape Road 2 is the sequel to the popular Escape Road, where you play as a bank robber driving away from the police force. Lots of new features have been added in this part!And if you are looking for a fast-paced driving and survival game, try https://survival-race.io, where you will enter the arena with a winged supercar to fight against many other players.

What is Escape Road 2?

Escape Road 2 is an action-packed driving game where you must evade the police after a bank robbery. It's a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, combining fast-paced driving with strategic decision-making.

How to Play Escape Road 2

Navigate the city streets using the arrow keys or WASD. Your goal is to escape the police by driving skillfully, dodging obstacles, and utilizing shortcuts. A key new feature is the ability to jump out of your car and hijack other vehicles by pressing the spacebar. This adds a strategic layer, allowing you to adapt to the situation and choose the best vehicle for your escape.

Features of Escape Road 2

  • Vehicle Hijacking: Jump out of your car and take control of other vehicles for a strategic advantage.
  • Enhanced Graphics: Experience the city and chases with improved visuals.
  • Dynamic City Environment: Navigate a complex urban landscape with unpredictable obstacles and shortcuts.
  • Relentless Police Pursuit: Outsmart and evade increasingly aggressive police forces.
  • Variety of Vehicles: Discover and utilize different vehicles with varying performance characteristics.
  • Improved Controls: Enjoy more responsive and intuitive driving controls.
